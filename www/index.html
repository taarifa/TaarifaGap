      About HOF

      Get To Know Us

      Hall of Fame (HoF) is an annual social-educational event organized by DAELIT Group and Mzumbe University Students. Hall of Fame aims to honour college students' efforts towards designing solutions for various problems facing their University Community and the Society at large. We recognize students who have taken their skills from class into the real world by designing solutions to address various issues.

      Objectives

      • To build a Platform for students at college level to utilize their knowledge and skills in improving their communities by supporting their already-established initiatives with seed funding.
      • To award students who have shown excellence in areas such as innovation, entrepreneurship, community service with tools such as mentorship, trainings and internships to advance their already-possesed skills.
      • To establish a special scholarship fund for students who have proven exellence in college but face financial constraints such as tuition fees and study resources in their pursuit of knowledge in college.

      Why go mobile ?

      Mobile and Tablets are slowly replacing laptops as laptops are replacing desktops - This being said, the Hall of Fame team decided on using this technology to create it's own mobile app with the help of GetCore Group.

      The Hall of Fame mobile application was build for the purpose of simplyfing the communication channel and reporting the needful information about the event.

      The App has features like Event Timer, Registration, Agenda, Testimonials and Finalists in it's initial version.

      The app will continue to be updated and have more features for the reason of keeping the participants, winners and prospective registrands in touch with Hall of Fame.
